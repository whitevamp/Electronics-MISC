Knob shaft diameters:
Full sized pots typically have 1/4 (0.250)" shafts and 3/8" busings, these are RV4, and larger ModPot styles.
They may also have 1/8" concentric shafts.

Miniature pots typically have 1/8 (0.125)" shafts, these are RV6, some Modpots and many miniature Clarostat
and AB type W and G pots. These may also have a concentric shaft, typically 0.078" in diameter.
