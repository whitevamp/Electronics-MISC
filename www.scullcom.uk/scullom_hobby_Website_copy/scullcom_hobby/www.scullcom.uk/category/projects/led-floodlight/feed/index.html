Circuit description and information on using the PT4115 integrated circuit to control LED current.
The 10 watt 12 volt LED (cost £1) and the PT4115 module (cost £2) can be found on eBay. For the PT4115 module search for “Car battery 12V ~ 24V constant current LED drive driver power supply 10W”.
